        COUNSEL EASE is an Online Mental Health Network designed to cater to the specific 
        challenges that colleges and college students face.

        At COUNSEL EASE, we're here to help young adults like you with the challenges of growing
        up and becoming more independent. We know you have a lot on your plate with classes,
        friends, and other responsibilities. Sometimes feeling unmotivated can be a sign of bigger
        issues like anxiety, feeling down, or struggling with learning. We're here to help you tap 
        into your own drive to take charge of your life and give you the tools to do it.

        Whether you're finding college tough, transitioning into a job, or figuring out what you 
        want for yourself, COUNSEL EASE can support you to grow and do well. Don't wait! Give us a 
        try today! And yes, we can still help you out during the summer or holiday breaks.

        We have different options for one-on-one sessions to fit into your busy college life. Our
        online platform is easy to use and keeps your conversations private, so you can talk from
        your dorm room using a secure video chat.
