Steps for Scheduling a Session

Step 1: Login with your Credentials.
Step 2: Select Date & Time for your session.
Step 3: Select the available Counsellor from the list.
Step 4: Confirm the counsellor details with Date & Time and Book Appointment.
Step 5: You will receive an email within 24 hours with all the details.
Step 6: You are done .Its that simple
